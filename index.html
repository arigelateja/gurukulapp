Founder's Note

A personal message from our founder

Founder Photo

Dear Parents and Families,

Welcome to Gurukul Preschool, where every child's journey begins with love, laughter, and learning. As the founder of this wonderful institution, I am deeply honored to share our story with you.

My journey in early childhood education began over 20 years ago when I realized that the foundation we provide in these crucial early years shapes the entire trajectory of a child's life. At Gurukul, we believe that every child is a unique star with unlimited potential waiting to shine.

Our approach combines the best of traditional values with modern educational practices. We understand that children learn best when they feel safe, loved, and encouraged to explore their world. That's why we've created an environment where curiosity is celebrated, creativity is nurtured, and every milestone is cherished.

What sets Gurukul apart is our commitment to seeing each child as an individual. We don't believe in one-size-fits-all education. Instead, we tailor our approach to meet each child where they are, helping them grow at their own pace while challenging them to reach new heights.

Our dedicated team of educators shares this vision and brings years of experience, passion, and love to every interaction with your child. Together, we create magical moments of discovery that will stay with your child forever.

Thank you for considering Gurukul for your child's early education journey. We look forward to partnering with you in nurturing your little star's bright future.

With warm regards,

Sarah Johnson
Founder & Director

Indian Culture & Language

Preserving heritage while embracing modern education

Celebrating Our Heritage

At Gurukul, we believe in the importance of cultural identity and language preservation. Our Indian Culture & Language program helps children connect with their roots while building confidence in their cultural heritage.

Hindi Language Learning

Interactive Hindi lessons with songs, stories, and games to make language learning fun and engaging for young minds.

Cultural Festivals

Celebrating major Indian festivals like Diwali, Holi, and Raksha Bandhan with traditional activities and stories.

Indian Music & Dance

Introduction to classical Indian music, folk songs, and traditional dance forms suitable for young children.

Indian Stories & Values

Food & Nutrition

Healthy, nutritious meals designed for growing children

Our Commitment to Healthy Eating

At Gurukul Preschool, we understand that proper nutrition is fundamental to a child's growth, development, and learning. Our carefully planned meals provide the essential nutrients children need to thrive both physically and mentally.

Fresh & Organic

We prioritize fresh, organic ingredients whenever possible, ensuring your child receives the highest quality nutrition.

Balanced Meals

Every meal is carefully balanced with proteins, carbohydrates, healthy fats, vitamins, and minerals.

Allergy Aware

We accommodate food allergies and dietary restrictions with safe, alternative meal options.

Child-Friendly

Meals are prepared in child-appropriate portions and textures, making eating enjoyable and safe.

Our Meal Planning Approach

Our certified nutritionist works closely with our kitchen staff to create weekly meal plans that are both nutritious and appealing to young children. We focus on:

  • Seasonal fruits and vegetables
  • Whole grains and lean proteins
  • Limited processed foods and sugars
  • Cultural diversity in meal options
  • Hydration with water and healthy beverages
