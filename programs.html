Preschool Program

Comprehensive early childhood education for ages 2.5 - 4 years

What We Offer

Our preschool program is designed to prepare your child for kindergarten while fostering a love for learning. We focus on developing essential skills through play-based learning and structured activities.

Pre-Reading Skills

Introduction to letters, sounds, and early literacy through interactive activities

Pre-Math Concepts

Numbers, counting, shapes, and basic mathematical thinking

Creative Arts

Painting, drawing, crafts, and creative expression activities

Physical Development

Gross and fine motor skills development through play

Indian Culture & Language

Preserving heritage while embracing modern education

Celebrating Our Heritage

At Gurukul, we believe in the importance of cultural identity and language preservation. Our Indian Culture & Language program helps children connect with their roots while building confidence in their cultural heritage.

Hindi Language Learning

Interactive Hindi lessons with songs, stories, and games to make language learning fun and engaging for young minds.

Cultural Festivals

Celebrating major Indian festivals like Diwali, Holi, and Raksha Bandhan with traditional activities and stories.

Indian Music & Dance

Introduction to classical Indian music, folk songs, and traditional dance forms suitable for young children.

Indian Stories & Values

Traditional Indian stories, fables, and moral lessons that teach important values and cultural wisdom.

Benefits of Cultural Education

Builds cultural pride and identity
Enhances cognitive development
Promotes family bonding
Prepares for global citizenship
